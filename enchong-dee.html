Enchong Dee Biography

Publish date: 2024-09-14

Dee who is age 28 , first started his career in 2006 and is active till now . Enchong Dee did his study from De La Salle University-Manila . 

After finishing Dee's studies he first came to the sight of media in when he appeared in a talk show Homeboy as one of the guests .He had then caught the eye of ads agency like Closeup , KFC and others .His contract with them finished on 2007 .

After his contract , his acting roles began and in 2009 he was added to the main cast of Katorse .

Enchong in August 18, 2012 won the one-million pesos briefcase in the episode of "Kapamilya Deal Or no Deal "

Enchong wanted to keep his relationship and affairs private . He did not disclose his girlfriend because he wanted to keep her away from critisims . He introduced his girlfriend to his fans in March 2014 during 8th star Magic ball . His girlfriend was Samantha Lewis and they confirmed that they broke up in 2016 but they are still good friends .

Dee's ethnicity is Filipino , Chinese and nationality Philippine.He has a net worth of $58 million.

Dee has started his career in 2006 and since then he has won numerous awards .Among the different awards he won some of them are Pinakapasadong Kabataan , Most promising team ,German Maren Youth Achievement Award , Star Magis Ball's Most Romantic Couple with Erich Gonzales ,Dance Hit of the year and others . His agency is star magic .

He has not only done different Tv series and flims ,he also has his own albums and recordings recorded in 2014 and 2016 which were "Enchong Dee " and "Enchong Dee Moves " . In addition to that he has also appeared in different Music Video .
