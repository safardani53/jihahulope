Hugh Grant Knocks Up Anna Elisabet Eberstein, Quietly Produces Third Love Child

Publish date: 2024-04-12

Anna Elisabet Eberstein is Hugh Grant’s baby mama. One of them, that is. This celebrity gossip was reported awhile back, but never confirmed until now.

Back in January, Grant had yet to publicly confirm or deny that he is the father of another love child with Anna Elisabet Eberstein, a Swedish TV producer.

Eberstein’s then-16-month-old son was born in September 2012.

However, many months later, the 54-year-old has finally broken his silence on being the father of Eberstein’s little boy, and even gushed about his son.

Grant, who was interviewed by The Daily Mail at a racetrack where he was driving a Ferrari in a charity race, told the reporter his son is doing "excellent."

"I love him very much," he added, noting that he "sees him most days, all the time," and that he’s not dating Anna: "We’re very good friends. She’s a great mother."

 

It was only revealed that Grant was the father of Eberstein’s child when the birth was re-registered in late 2013, and Grant was named on the birth certificate.

Interestingly, Eberstein was pregnant at the same time as Tinglan Hong, who gave birth to her second child with Grant, a son named Felix, in December 2012.

Hong and Grant welcomed a daughter, Tabitha, back in 2011.

Congratulations to Hugh and both women on the kiddos!
