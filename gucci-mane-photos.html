Publish date: 2024-04-19

Gucci Mane is a famous rapper and record executive. He released his first album, ‘Trap House,’ in 2005, followed by ‘Hard To Kill’ in 2006. His third album, Back to the Trap House,’ came out in 2007. In total, Gucci has now released 15 albums, his most recent being ‘Ice Daddy,’ which came out in June 2021. He’s collaborated with numerous artists such as Drake, Chris Brown, Mariah Carey, Bruno Mars, and more. Gucci spent time in prison from 2014 to 2016 on several charges, including firearm posession by a convicted felon and disorderly conduct.

Gucci is currently married to model and entreprenuer Keyshia Ka’Oir. They have a son together. Gucci also has a son from his relationship with ex Sheena Evans.
