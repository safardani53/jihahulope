Publish date: 2024-03-21

ANNA WOOLHOUSE has been labelled "absolutely stunning" by darts fans as she hosted coverage of the PDC World Darts Championship.

The Sky Sports host presented action from Wednesday's afternoon session at Alexandra Palace.

Woolhouse returned to TV screens as the tournament resumed with the start of the third round.

She stunned viewers with her daring outfit as she paired a brown leather skirt and long sleeve white top.

Her Instagram followers were also treated to a selfie from the Sky Sports studio as Woolhouse showed off her outfit with the caption: "Back in the Fun House!"

Meanwhile, fans on X, formerly Twitter, praised her bold outfit choice.
