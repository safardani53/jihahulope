Noelle Scaggs is an American musician, singer and songwriter from Los Angeles, known as the lead co-singer of pop group Fitz and the Tantrums. Scaggs has also had numerous collaborations with Black Eyed Peas, Dilated Pe People, Quantic, Mayer Hawthorne, and Damian Marley. In 2008, she became the co-singer of indie pop and neo-soul groups Fitz and the Tantrums, and appeared on their 2010 album Pickin 'Up the Pieces, reaching No.1 on the Billboard Heatseekers charts. She has toured extensively with the band and performed in shows such as Jimmy Kimmel Live !, Conan, The Tonight Show with Jay Leno, Ellen, GMA, The Tonight Show with Jimmy Fallon and The Late Late Show with James Corden. She was highly appreciated by critics for her "powerful voice" and her charisma on stage.
