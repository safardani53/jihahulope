    How do you defrost a fridge freezer?

    The simplest and easiest way to defrost your fridge freezer is to unplug it and leave the door open and let the ice melt on its own.

    It can take a few hours to let the ice melt naturally - depending on how thick the ice is.

    How do you defrost a fridge freezer without turning it off?

    If you want to defrost your freezer without turning the fridge off, many appliances have a dial in the freezer compartment that controls the chill factor.

    If you turn the chill factor in your freezer to zero, you have basically turned it off.

    Remove the food from your freezer and put it in the fridge or an ice box while you defrost the freezer.

    You may want to speed up the process with a bowl of boiling water producing steam.

    There are also de-icing products available such as Fresh & Bright fridge freezer de-icer spray or HG freezer de-icer - both available from Amazon.

    How long do fridge freezers last?

    We would expect a fridge freezer to last at least ten years.

    How do you get rid of a fridge freezer?

    Many retailers offer a service where they will collect your old fridge freezer when they deliver your new one.

    AO also offer a collection and recycling service for freezers - find out more.

    If not, be sure to check if your local council offers a collection service for recycling used white goods.
