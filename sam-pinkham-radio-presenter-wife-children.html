Who is Sam Pinkham and is he married?

Publish date: 2024-08-18

SAM PINKHAM has been a regular on radio airwaves for over two decades and has a number of TV credits to his name.

The star, who also has a famous sibling, has had his family by his side throughout his rise to success.

Who is Sam Pinkham?

Sam Pinkham is a radio and television presenter from Buckinghamshire, England.

He has had an impressive career in broadcasting since he began working in the early 2000s.

Prior to embarking on his presenting career, Sam worked in the world of property for a decade.

He isn't the only member of his family to carve out a career in media as his sister is Sky Sports F1 presenter Natalie Pinkham.

While growing up, the famous siblings' mother worked as a barrister while their father was a property developer.

Natalie and Sam appear to have a very close relationship and frequently appear on each other's social media pages.

According to his profile on Arlington Talent, Sam loves to compete in physical challenges.

As well as running marathons, competing on a national level at Cross Fit and cycling the length of Britain, the star is a keen skier, climber and scuba-diver.

Is Sam Pinkham married?

Sam is married to wife Kathryn Pinkham.

She is the founder of The Insomnia Clinic which, according to its official website, is one of the UK's only specialist insomnia service.

In August 2023, Sam posted a heartwarming tribute to his wife on Instagram for her 40th birthday.

Alongside a lovely photo of the couple on the beach, Sam wrote: "Happy bday to this absolutely fantastic human being.

"You light up every room, every friendship and every gathering".

He then jokingly added: "You look bloody amazing for 40 until [tomorrow] at 10am when you [sky] dive out of a plane and splat all over Hampshire".

Does Sam Pinkham have any children?

Sam shares a large family with his wife Kathryn.

The couple have two daughters, named Evie and Nina, and a young son called Kit.

All of Sam's kids regularly appear on his Instagram page as he often shares snaps from their travels as a family.

Sam's son, Kit, celebrated his first birthday in October 2023.

Pinkham posted a series of adorable pictures of his boy to mark the occasion, captioning the series: "Happy first birthday Kit Pinkham you gorgeous little fella.

"Loved every minute of the last year getting to know you.

"I've [got] your back for the next 20 years if you've got mine for the 20 after that. I love you."

What TV and radio shows has Sam Pinkham starred in?

Sam got his start in media by working in radio.

He began as a traffic reporter for stations like Virgin Radio, Northants 96, and Leicester Sound, before joining Century 106 in 2002 as a weekend breakfast presenter.

Later, at the same station, Sam would score the coveted drive-time slot and in 2006, he began fronting the flagship weekday breakfast programme.

Sam is perhaps best known for his work on Gem 106, formerly known as Heart East Midlands.

He worked there for 11 years, presenting the Sam and Amy breakfast show with co-host Amy Voce.

Both Pinkham and Voce left Gem 106 in September 2017 to join Virgin Radio UK.

Sam has also had a lucrative career in television.

He is a host on Channel 5's Holiday Homes in the Sun, alongside Amanda Lamb and JLS star JB Gill, and has also presented children's science show The Big Bang for CITV in the past.

Holiday Homes in the Sun began in September 2022 and, according to Channel 5, sees Pinkham, Lamb and Gill go head-to-head as they "travel the length and breadth of Europe and the UK to find the very best holiday rentals".
