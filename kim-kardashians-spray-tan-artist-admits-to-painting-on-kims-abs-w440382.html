Kim Kardashians Spray Tan Artist Admits to Painting on Kims Abs

Publish date: 2024-09-07

Abs-tastic! Kim Kardashian’s spray tan artist admitted to “painting” on the reality star’s abs in a Facebook post on Friday, September 16.

“While I was spray tanning Kim between layers as she likes to get dark she would go outside and took this snapchat while drying, have to admit spray tan looks pretty awesome on her and even painted her abs while spraying her!!” Sobe Tan co-owner Fabiola Trujillo wrote, along with the Harper’s Bazaar article about Kardashian’s stunning naked selfie.

On Thursday, September 15, the Keeping Up With the Kardashians star, 35, shared a Snapchat video of herself in the nude, showing off her freshly sprayed figure.

“I just did a midnight spray tan, you guys,” she says in the video clip. “Tanorexic.”

In the video, the Selfish author stands in her Miami hotel room with her arms and a strategically placed chair covering her breasts and nether regions.

The reality star made headlines in March after she posted a similarly provocative nude mirror selfie to her Instagram account, prompting celebrities and fans alike to weigh in on her no-holds-barred sharing style.

“I was about to get in the shower; I took a nude selfie, kept it in my phone for over a year,” she told Harper’s Bazaar in July of the controversial post. “I just like the picture, so I was like, ‘Let me put censor bars on it and post it.’ I don’t do things to be like, ‘This is powerful. I’m going to show you guys that this is my “message.”‘ I’m not that type of person. I’m empowered by it, but I’m not doing it specifically to show power.”

Kardashian’s husband, Kanye West, has been supportive of his love’s sexy social media presence. In the same issue, he told the magazine that he thinks the mother of two’s nude selfies are “beautiful.”

“I love the ones from the side, the back ones and the front. I just love seeing her naked; I love nudity. And I love beautiful shapes,” the 39-year-old rapper said. “I feel like it’s almost a Renaissance thing, a painting, a modern version of a painting. I think it’s important for Kim to have her figure. To not show it would be like Adele not singing.”
