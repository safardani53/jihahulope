I'm A Celeb's Jessica Plummer bakes with adorable daughter in lockdown as she prepares to head to Wa

Publish date: 2024-02-15

I'm a Celeb's Jessica Plummer squeezed in some quality time with her adorable daughter before she heads to Wales for the show.

The EastEnders star, 28, enjoyed an evening of baking sweet treats with her four-year-old daughter, Noa.

Jessica shared a clip of her making the cupcakes which she joked was not how she imagined.

She wrote alongside the clip: "We've gone for a simple cupcake recipe, surely this can't go wrong."

Sharing a snap of the inside of her fluffy cupcakes, she said: "I take it back, do not judge a book by its cover, it's actually incredible."

The proud mum often shares cute clips of the pair dancing and singing around the house.

The actress recently posted the photos to social media of her and Noa beaming for the camera with the caption: “Long as I’m making her proud" alongside a crown emoji.

The TV star has singed up for this years series of I'm a Celebrity and recently revealed that she plans to "gain weight" before the show.

It appears that she is preparing her body by bulking up ahead of being forced to live on rations.

Taking to her Instagram Story, Jessica filmed herself working out alongside Noa.

She captioned the first energetic clip: "Tryna put on weight and get thicker this winter…"

Dressed in a sports bra and leggings, the actress' toned stomach and lithe body is on full display.

After her workout, which included squats and stretches, Jessica shared one of 19-year-old Maisie Smith's gym bunny snaps to her Story as her inspiration.

Maisie is famed for her enviable muscles, with the Strictly star regularly showing off her strong limbs and rippling abs on social media.
