Photos Of The Rapper Hollywood Life

Publish date: 2024-04-23

Lil Baby (real name Dominique Armani Jones) is a rapper, singer and songwriter that hails from Atlanta, Georgia. The 25-year-old rose to fame rose to fame in 2017 when he released his initial mixtape called Perfect Timing that featured other rising stars including Young Thug and Lil Yachty.

Singles from that mixtape, including “Freestyle” and “All of a Sudden” greatly helped catapult his career. That momentum carried into his debut 2018 studio album Harder Than Ever which debuted at number 3 on the Billboard Hot 100.

Lil Baby also did a collaborative mixtape with fellow emcee Gunna called Drip Harder in 2018. The track “Drip Too Hard” earned them both a Grammy nomination in 2020.

He scored big at the 2019 BET Awards the following year by winning Best New Artist over other big time names including Blueface, City Girls and the late Juice Wrld.
