Publish date: 2024-06-08

KATIE Price braved foot pain to go Christmas shopping with boyfriend Carl Woods and daughter Princess in her latest YouTube video.

The mum-of-five is still recovering from a freak accident that saw her break both feet while on holiday in Turkey in August.

She managed to spend some time out of her wheelchair, as she enjoyed a private browse around the Van Hage garden centre in Hertfordshire.

Carl's mum put in a word with management, and the couple were allowed after hours access to the store.

Katie treated her eldest son Harvey to a rainbow tree and glittery frog decorations; the amphibious creatures are his favourite.

The 42-year-old Facetimed her son to show him the decs, and then teased her next video would show them putting up, and dressing, the colourful tree.

Standing upright, Katie said: "I'm lucky. I'm walking like that for the first time in sliders.

"They have told me they have got wheelchairs, and I've said I'm gonna need it because the shop's so big, I'm not going to be able to walk that far."

After finding the glittery frog baubles, she said: "When Harvey sees all this he's going to absolutely nuts. I can't wait. It's going to be the cutest thing ever."

In the video, Princess, 13, can be seen wearing makeup on her face.

Her mum faced a backlash from fans over the weekend for giving the teen a heavy makeover.
