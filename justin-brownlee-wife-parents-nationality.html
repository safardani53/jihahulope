The professional basketball player Justin Brownlee is a married man with four children.

However, due to his preference for keeping his personal life private, it is unclear to many whether he is still married or not.

On top of that, Justin rarely shares any pictures or information about his family with the public, which adds to the mystery surrounding his personal life.

Justin Brownlee is an American-born Filipino professional basketball player who plays for the Barangay Ginebra San Miguel in the Philippine Basketball Association (PBA).

Before playing professionally, Justin played college basketball at St. John’s University from 2009 to 2011.

After not being selected in the 2011 NBA draft, Brownlee embarked on his professional career in the NBA Development League, playing for the Maine Red Claws and Erie BayHawks.

In 2016, he joined Barangay Ginebra San Miguel in the PBA. During his time with Barangay Ginebra, he achieved significant success, winning six conference titles, including four PBA Governors’ Cups and two PBA Commissioner’s Cups.

Brownlee also had a stint with San Miguel Alab Pilipinas in the ASEAN Basketball League, where he clinched the 2017–18 season championship.

Later, in 2023, Brownlee obtained Filipino citizenship and played basketball, representing the Philippines national basketball team.

He then won a gold medal at the 2023 Southeast Asian Games.
