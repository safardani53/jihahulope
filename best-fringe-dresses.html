12 Best Fringe Dresses 2021 | The Sun UK

Publish date: 2024-05-24

SWISH, swoosh: texture and movement is hot on our radar, so we've picked out the best fringe dresses to add some drama to your wardrobe this season.

Fun and glamorous, the fringe dress is a style that doesn't take itself too seriously - exactly the sort of garment we're looking to now that the lockdown is lifted and we can actually show off the clothes we're wearing under our previously alfresco-only layers.

Forever associated with the 1920s flapper aesthetic, the fringe dress was made famous by designers including Madeline Vionette and 'father of haute couture', Charles Worth—the latter of whom created the iconic silver fringed tinsel number we've come to know and love.

Whether the original flapper girls did in fact frequently wear fringe dresses is up for debate though: many claim it was Hollywood's late-1950s interpretation of the twenties that cemented the look: think film classics like Singin’ In the Rain and Some Like It Hot.

Regardless of its exact origin, the fringe dress has stood the test of time and was seen all over the Autumn/Winter 2020 runways: from Prada’s tassel-trimmed black column dresses to Burberry's silver sequinned midis. The trend has continued into this season, with a plethora of fringed beauties available to shop now that are just perfect for soaring summer temperatures—festive season not needed.

Check out our faves below and see which fringe takes your fancy.
