Brody Jenner Hollywood Life

Publish date: 2024-06-15

Bio:
Brody Jenner (born Sam Brody Jenner on August 21, 1983 in Malibu, California), is a reality TV star, model and DJ. He is best known as Bruce Jenner’s son on Keeping Up With The Kardashians. He became the stepbrother of Kim, Khloe, and Kourtney Kardashian in 1991. However, his father and Kris Jenner split in 2015. Kendall and Kylie Jenner are Brody’s half-sisters. Brody first appeared on 2005’s The Princes of Malibu with his brother, Brandon, and his friend Spencer Pratt. He would later star with Spencer on MTV’s The Hills. He created his own reality show, Bromance in 2009, where he tried to find his new best friend. He has modeled for Guess and Agent Provocateur.

Best Known For:
Brody Jenner is best known for appearing on Keeping Up With The Kardashians and from being associated with his famous relatives.

Personal Life:
Brody Jenner is the son of Bruce Jenner and Linda Thompson. He famously dated Lauren Conrad from 2006-2007, which led to the deterioration of his longtime friendship with Spencer Pratt. In 2010 he was romantically linked to singer Avril Lavigne. He is currently engaged to his longtime girlfriend Kaitlynn Carter. Jenner proposed to the blogger while the two were on a vacation to Sumba island in Indonesia in May 2016. Brody and Kaitlynn have been dating since 2014.
