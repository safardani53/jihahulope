BOOTS is giving away 2000 designer Yves Saint Laurent Beauté Fragrance Case for free this month, and yes, this is for real.

This fabulous giveaway is part of Boots' 'Big Deal' promotion, where the high street beauty haven launch weekly drops of unmissable offers on must have brands.
