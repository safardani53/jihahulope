What happened to influencer Lexi Reed?

Publish date: 2024-09-15

INFLUENCER Lexi Reed spent a month in the hospital earlier this year due to kidney failure.

In April 2022, the star then announced that she was back in the hospital, and fans want to know why.

Who is influencer Lexi Reed?

Lexi Reed rose to fame in 2016 after documenting her remarkable weight loss journey on her Instagram account, @fatgirlfedup, in an effort to lose 312 pounds with her husband, Danny, who lost 95 pounds.

In an interview with People in 2018, Reed said, "A friend challenged us to 30 days of no eating out, cheat meals, soda or alcohol. 

"We started cooking, meal prepping, tracking our calories, and reading nutrition labels."

Reed updated her followers on her health on February 15, 2022, telling them she was on dialysis after being put in a medically-induced coma.

She started explaining what the new normal looks like for her with a photo on Instagram of her going through dialysis.

"Not my best 6am photo but part of my daily ritual - dialysis," she said. Reed was in dialysis three times every week to rid her body of the excess fluids that caused her organ failure.

What did Lexi Reed say after getting out of the hospital?

Reed announced she was home in an Instagram post on March 21, 2022, saying, "I'm officially off of dialysis & port free!"

She told her 1.2million followers that she is "grateful for all this body has done for me despite the good and bad times.

"I still have a long way to go in this new part of my journey with my health, struggles, and even some unanswered questions - but happy to still be going in the right direction after such a scare this year."

Reed set up a GoFundMe page to help with her medical bills and received $70k, but critics said she should have been able to afford health insurance.

People commented on her Instagram post asking for donations to her Support Lexi Reed in her Medical Expenses GoFundMe page claiming the influencer had a high net worth.

"People seem to think I'm some sort of millionaire just because the internet says so but we're far from that," Reed told Insider.

Although the website AllFamousBirthday claims Reed has a net worth of $1.5million, she said, "People on the internet are reading that I have all kinds of money, which is insane to me, because I clearly don't." 

She added, "Our house isn't even paid off and we only drive one vehicle so I'm not quite sure why people are so quick to believe anything these days."
