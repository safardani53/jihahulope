These Disney Movies Are Leaving Netflix in June!

Publish date: 2024-09-27

Let’s get down to business: Mulan is leaving Netflix, and it feels worse than training to defeat the Huns.

Disney lovers beware, because a few of your favorite movies might be getting axed from the streaming service in June. Those getting cut alongside the 1998 hit Mulan (sorry, Mushu!) include Hercules (1997), The Hunchback of Notre Dame (1996) and The Hunchback of Notre Dame II (2002).

Other feel-good flicks are getting booted too. Come next month, say goodbye to non-Disney films such as The Sisterhood of the Traveling Pants (2005), Bridget Jones’s Diary (2001) and About a Boy (2002).
