Prince Williams old air ambulance is being used to advertise a ladyboys hangout in Cambodia

Publish date: 2024-07-23

PRINCE William’s old air ambulance is being used to advertise a ladyboys’ hangout in Cambodia.

The chopper is on a poster promoting “all beautiful girls”, free pool and 24-hour happy hour at the Helicopter Bar.

The yellow aircraft was owned by East Anglian Air Ambulance which the Duke of Cambridge flew for from 2015 to 2017.

Visitors describe the bar in capital Phnom Penh’s red light district as full of topless women and prostitutes. One posted seeing “stunners” there and young women dancing raunchily to electronic music.

A second called it “another hooker bar” with “a ladyboy in the corner playing pool”.

A third said: “It’s the bar with tarts hovering around.”

Air ambulance chiefs tweeted a joke disclaimer over the ad on a three-wheel taxi.

They said: “We are in no way associated with the helicopter bar that is promoted on the back of this tuk tuk in Cambodia...but we wouldn’t say no to free pool.”
