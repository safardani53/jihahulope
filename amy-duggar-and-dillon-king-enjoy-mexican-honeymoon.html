Amy Duggar and Dillon King Enjoy Mexican Honeymoon

Publish date: 2024-07-21

They’re definitely not in Arkansas anymore.

Amy (Duggar) King and new husband Dillon King are taking full advantage of all Cancun has to offer on their luxurious Mexican honeymoon.

The couple has been actively documenting their romantic adventure since landing in the popular vacation destination on Wednesday.

“Lunch time with my wife,” Dillon wrote on Instagram, Friday, and added the hashtags,”#honeymoonkings #ringsfortheKings #DillandDuggsforever.”

In the panoramic photo, Amy poses at their lunch table nestled near a crystal-clear pool.

Amy also posted several trip photos on Friday, most of which featured a cool, tropical beverage.

In another image from the couple’s lunch, she slurps down a red “spritzer” from underneath a large, black straw hat.

“I love being his bride,” she wrote of an earlier photo featuring a pair of bridal flip flops and a pineapple-adorned drink.
