Big Brother's Nikki Grahame slammed for 'naked-shaming' topless female neighbour and posting it onli

Publish date: 2024-08-10

BIG Brother star Nikki Grahame has been blasted for shaming her neighbour and posting a video of the topless woman on Instagram.

The 38-year-old reality star took a video of the woman, who she spotted was topless in the flat opposite as she was getting changed.

TV personality Nikki, who found fame in 2006 after appearing in series seven of Big Brother, shared a nine-second clip to social media.

Alongside a shocked emoji, she captioned the video posted to her 91,000 followers: "Interesting views today".

Hastily deleting the clip, Nikki faced backlash from fans who were horrified she had shared the clip of the woman, who was totally unaware.

"Nikki posted an Instagram story of her topless neighbour and laughed at her," one horrified follower said. "She quickly deleted it.

"It was deleted within 10 minutes. But it was too late - thousands of people must have seen the video of this poor woman.

"I was shocked someone with this type of platform would violate a women's privacy in her own home."

Nikki shot to telly fame 15 years ago with her stint as a housemate in the Big Brother house at the peak of the show's popularity.

She was famed for her romance with fellow star Pete Bennett and her diary room rants about newcomer Susie Verrico where she repeatedly said: “Who is she?”
