Jisoo Attracted Notice For Her New Bleached Hairstyle While Attending Paris Fashion Week 2022

Publish date: 2024-04-26

Jisoo of BLACKPINK turned heads with her freshly bleached hair. The legendary K-pop singer was seen at Incheon International Airport, and her images quickly went viral on the internet. Despite being on vacation for a lengthy period, members of the group never fail to generate news by breaking records. The girls have been modeling and working with several fashion companies on their spring and autumn 2022 collections.

BLACKPINK Jisoo’s new hairstyle and airport ensemble for Paris Fashion Week 2022

Jisoo attracted notice for her new bleached hair color on March 1, 2022, while going to Incheon International Airport as Dior’s envoy to attend the Paris Fashion Week 2022 event. The star headed to her official Instagram account to show off her new hairdo and stylish clothes. Jisoo may be seen wearing a black cardigan with a matching black and white Christian Dior jumper on her Instagram stories.

Jisoo accessorized with the French fashion mogul’s current black handbag line and opened her poker-straight hair, bleached blonde from the bottom half. She wore the top part of her dress with dark blue denim pants and black stiletto boots. Soon after witnessing Jisoo’s new hair color and sophisticated airport dress, admirers went to Twitter to express their delight. They thought the idol’s unique style was perfect, and we’re excited to see her represent Dior at Paris Fashion Week 2022.

BLACKPINK’s most recent updates

BLACKPINK’s Jennie debuted her next collaboration with Gentle Monster on February 10, 2022. Following the successful debut of her entire project, Jentle Home, in 2021, the K-pop diva announced her next partnership, Jentle Garden. The singer shared images of her latest project on her official Instagram account.

Jennie shared videos and photos of a bouquet of bright orange and yellow flowers in a recyclable bag. The arrangement of flowers was placed on a lavender trolley, which she pushed to demonstrate to followers how it was done. Jennie concluded the film by revealing her most recent endeavor and sending her creative product to five K-celebrities.
