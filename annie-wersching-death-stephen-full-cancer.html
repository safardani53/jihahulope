Publish date: 2024-09-13

Latest The Last of Us episode honors Annie Wersching

The official Twitter account for Naughty Dog Central, the production company behind The Last of Us, shared ahead of Sunday's episode that it's dedicated to Annie Wersching.

The tweet read, “Tonight’s episode will be dedicated to Annie Wersching.

"Her name will be added to the episode later this week.”

Actress Julie Gonzalo adds to tributes

Actress Julie Gonzalo has taken to Twitter to honor her castmate Annie Wersching.

The two appeared in the show, TNT drama, Dallas.

Oh dear Annie. I remember what a beautiful light you were on the set of DALLAS. Rest In Peace now… #AnnieWersching

— Julie Gonzalo (@julie_gonzalo) January 30, 2023

TVD fans remember Annie

Fans of the hit show, The Vampire Diaries are remembering Annie Wersching from her time on the series.

One fan shared photos of her on set to Twitter and wrote, “You’ll always be Mama Salvatore to the TVD fandom. Any fan easily can remember how interactive she was with us during her time on the show.

How excited she was to be in this role and her genuine good heart. Annie Wersching rest peacefully now.”

Abigail Spencer honors her castmate

Abigail Spencer, best known for her roles in Timeless and Suites has taken to Instagram to honor Annie Wersching.

The two shared scenes together on the NBC series, Timeless.

She wrote alongside a photo of Annie at a table read, “My darling @anniewersching. The best scene partner. My arch #Timeless nemesis where we could barely keep a straight face.

This is a devastating loss to our community. She was the backbone of many of your favorite shows & ours. The ringer.

When you needed someone to come in and kick ass or elevate a story line or your life line: call Annie. This is so hard… Steve… no words. Sending you & the boys heaps of love & support.

Please, please #Timeless fans if you can donate to the #gofundme page started by @officialevercarradine please do. Link in bio. Her family needs all the love and support we can generate. Her spirit is all around and transcends time & space.”
