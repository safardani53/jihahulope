Photos With His Kids, Wives, and More Hollywood Life

Publish date: 2024-09-05

Robert De Niro is a two-time Oscar-winning actor, who’s starred in a wide variety of movies throughout his prolific career. Check out photos of him with his family through the years.

Robert De Niro is one of the most celebrated movie actors of all time. After making his early breakouts in critically acclaimed films like ‘The Godfather Part II’, ‘Taxi Driver’ and ‘Raging Bull,’ he’s been incredibly celebrated throughout his career. He’s also an incredibly versatile actor, able to perform both serious dramas like in ‘Silver Linings Playbook’, crime thrillers, and even comedies like in ‘Meet the Parents.’

Throughout his career, Robert has been married twice. His first wife was Diahnne Abbott from 1976 to 1988. The pair share a son Raphael and a daughter Drena. He was wed to Grace Hightower from 1997 until announcing their separation in 2018. They share two kids, Elliot and Helen. Robert also has two sons Julian and Aaron from his ex-girlfriend Toukie Smith. He also announced on May 9, 2023, that he gave birth to his 7th child in 2023 but didn’t share his child’s mother.
