Im a backwoods Barbie with blue hair and piercings who loves riding horses - Im just

Publish date: 2024-09-23

A BLUE-HAIRED country gal's appearance isn't as pure as her crops.

The self-acclaimed "backwoods Barbie" showed viewers her look, piercings and all.

In a recent video, TikToker Desert Huntress (@officialdeserthuntress) danced with her beloved animal.

Her hair was long and died a bright blue to match her spikey nails.

She wore a mustard-colored beanie, oversized flannel, and hoodie.

Desert Huntress gripped onto the horse lead and twirled from side to side.

The devoted country girl sported winged black eyeliner and fake eyelashes.

Her face was pierced all over, including four around her mouth, on both cheeks, nose, and forehead.

"I'm just a backwoods Barbie," the music sang.

Desert Huntress flashed a wide smile and mouthed the words.
