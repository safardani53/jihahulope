Career

Sonu Gowda made her Kannada film debut as Namana in the film “Inthi Ninna Preethiya.” Additionally, she collaborated with actor Srinagar Kitty on her 2008 debut film. In the same year, she starred as Lakshmi in Mahesh Babu’s Kannada film “Paramesha Panwala.” She made a cameo appearance in Raam and played Priyanka in the 2009 film “Gulama.”

Following that, she starred as Anitha in the bilingual film “Police Quarters,” released in 2010 in Tamil and Kannada. She made her Tamil film debut in the same year as a Nurse in the film “Sivappu Mazhai.”

In the same year, she made her Malayalam film debut as Savithri in the film “Best Actor.” Additionally, she made a cameo appearance as Doctor Beena in the Malayalam film “Doubles” in 2011.

In the same year, she starred as the Yamuna in the Tamil film “Aanmai Thavarael.” In 2013, she starred in the Kannada film “Dyavre” as Shruthi Kalappa. Similarly, in 2014, she starred as Thenmozhi in the Tamil film “Amara” and as Meghna in the Malayalam film “1 by Two.”
