April Love Geary Posts Baby Bump Snap in Her Underwear

Publish date: 2024-06-15

April Love Geary can’t wait to meet her second little bundle of joy!

The 24-year-old model, who’s engaged to musician Robin Thicke, showed off her baby bump in a mirror selfie late Wednesday night.

Dressed only in a black bra and matching high-waisted panties, with her long dark hair hang loose over her shoulders, Geary pursed her lips for the camera as she cradled her bare belly for the snapshot.

“Two week countdown 🌟,” she wrote.

The new snap comes almost three weeks after Geary shared one of herself in red boy short-style underwear, pulling up a white T-shirt to reveal her bump. She captioned it, “Ready to POP, lock n drop this baby outta me.”

Geary announced the couple’s exciting baby news on Instagram in August, sharing an adorable video of their daughter Mia Love, 2 this month, holding her mom’s ultrasound photos. She originally shared that the baby was due on Thicke’s 42nd birthday: March 10.

In a series of clips and photos shared to Geary’s Instagram story days later, she and Thicke attended a sex “Reveal Dinner” at CATCH restaurant in Los Angeles with a custom menu, complete with a four-layer cake.

When the mom-to-be cut into the confection, a liquid pink center poured out to the cheers of their guests as the musician high-fived multiple people.

After revealing her second baby on the way with the “Blurred Lines” singer, Geary shared on Instagram that she had suffered a miscarriage before the couple welcomed Mia. (Thicke is also dad to son Julian Fuego, 8½.)

“I hear about miscarriages and it scared me. [Were] you scared or [are] you still scared?” one fan asked using the app’s questions feature.

“I had one before Mia,” the model shared. “But I’m not scared, worrying or stressing out too much isn’t good for the baby. Life happens and we can’t control everything.”
