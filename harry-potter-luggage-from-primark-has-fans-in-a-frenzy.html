Harry Potter luggage from Primark has fans in a frenzy

Publish date: 2024-04-10

HARRY Potter fans are loving the new Hogwarts-approved luggage from bargain retailer Primark.

At just £12 the rucksack and satchel come complete with badges Harry, Ron and Hermione would be proud of.

Fans of JK Rowling's wizarding world are never short of choice in Primark with everything from pyjamas, to flipflops, mugs and bedding all available.

Fans are loving the bags which feature patch-style badges with references to the Harry Potter story.

Hogwarts crests adorn both bags along with a 'HP' Harry Potter logo.

The rucksack features Hedwig, Harry's pet owl while the satchel features a dramatic lightening strike, to represent Harry's forehead scar.

With fans like Marshdmello exclaiming "a bit in love with the backpack" there were over 50,000 comments on the Instagram post within hours of it going up.

Some people already had the bag and others needed it to complete Harry Potter collections.
