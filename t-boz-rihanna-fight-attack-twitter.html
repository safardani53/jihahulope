T-Boz & Rihanna Fight: She Slams RiRi For Immature Twitter Attack

TMZ caught up with T-Boz at Bootsy Bellows on June 26, and grilled her on her much-buzzed-about Rihanna diss.

“I wasn’t talking about her then, and I ain’t talking about her now,” T-Boz replied. “That’s the real of it. I ain’t even got time to waste on that.”

The photog then asked about their Twitter exchange, and T-Boz replied, “No, there was just her exchange. I don’t do Twitter. I’m a grown-ass woman.”

Although T-Boz promised that she was “not a hater” and that she wished RiRi “the best, always,” she also said that if the star has anything more to say to her, she should say it to her face!

“You wanna holler, you know where to find me,” T-Boz challenged RiRi. “It ain’t hard.”
