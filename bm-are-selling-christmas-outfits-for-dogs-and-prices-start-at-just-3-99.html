B&amp;M are selling Christmas outfits for dogs and prices start at just 3.99

Publish date: 2024-08-01

B&M are selling Christmas outfits for dogs and prices start at just £3.99.

The budget retailer unveiled the festive selection of canine fancy dress outfits on its website earlier this week.

From a cute Christmas pudding costume to a sassy Mrs. Claus, there's an array of looks to ensure your furry friend looks seriously festive come December.

The website states: "Christmas is for pets; not just for humans.

"Give your pet the Christmas it deserves with our range of toys, treats and novelty gifts for cats, dogs and small pets.

"Dress your dog up at Christmas this year and make them look as festive as possible in a cute little outfit."

The costumes are available in different styles and sizes - ranging from extra-small to extra-large.

The Christmas togs won't break the bank either, as they start at a purse-friendly £3.99.
