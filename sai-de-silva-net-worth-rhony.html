Who is Sai De Silva from RHONY?

Publish date: 2024-06-12

THE TEAM behind the Real Housewives franchise came to a decision to reboot one of their shows.

As the previous Bravolebrities who were on RHONY pursue other endeavors, Sai De Silva is one of the new faces to join the reality program.

Who is Sai De Silva?

Born in Los Angeles and raised in New York City, Sai De Silva is a fashion influencer, style vlogger, and TV personality who operates from the latter.

Prior to her stint on television, Sai amassed a social media following through YouTube and Instagram.

On Sunday, October 16, 2022, it was announced that Sai was cast in season 14 of The Real Housewives of New York City.

The reboot of the series premiered on July 16, 2023.

What is Sai De Silva's net worth?

According to several outlets, Sai has an estimated net worth of around $1million.

On YouTube, her channel consists of over 50K subscribers, while on Instagram she has over 383K followers.

Her content consists of shopping hauls, beauty tutorials, and behind-the-scenes peeks into New York Fashion Week.

Sai also runs the lifestyle and fashion website Scout The City.

Similar to her other online platforms, Scout the City is made up of content across food, travel, and fitness.

At that year's BravoCon, franchise producer Andy took the stage to reveal the news.

Regarding the casting news, Sai teased her followers and said: "Scrolling and catching up on all the tea, have you heard?"

Is Sai married?

On June 16, 2009, Sai and her husband David Craig tied the knot.

The two usually remain hush-hush about the details of their relationship but stated on the former's website: "He likes his privacy. In a world where we share everything, it’s nice to have something that is private in my life."

In relation to David, she added: "However, he is very involved when it comes to his family and I think he should win an award for father of the year!"

The couple has two children together, a son named Rio Dash and a daughter named London Scout - hence the title of her platform.

How can I watch RHONY?

All previous editions of the program are available to stream on Peacock.
