Sister Wives star Janelle Brown shares diet secret after 100-lb. weight loss in new video

Publish date: 2024-10-07

JANELLE Brown has divulged her new diet secret after dropping over 100 pounds in her weight loss journey.

The Sister Wives star has been sharing progress photos and videos as she focuses on her health.

Now Janelle, 54, has given fans an inside peek at her latest diet secret with a new video shared on her Instagram Stories.

The reality star took to the platform on Saturday night to update her followers after she returned from a road trip with her daughter Savanah, 18.

The TV personality filmed her kitchen countertop covered in supplies to make a homemade pizza.

Janelle filmed her uncooked but prepped pizza creation with crust, sauce, mozzarella cheese, and pepperoni before panning right to show off the ingredients.

On the counter sat a box of cauliflower pizza crust, a low-carb alternative to normal pizza crust.

The TLC star also showed a bag of fat-free mozzarella cheese, sauce, and pepperoni.

Janelle explained: "I've had these crusts forever and so I'm going to try it.

"I'm really tired today - I guess it's all the traveling. Anyway, so all I have is fat-free mozzarella, we'll see how this turns out.

"But I knew if I ordered a whole pizza, I'd be in trouble. So we're going to try this and see how it goes."

Janelle followed up her video with a snapshot of the final cooked pizza, looking crispy and delicious.

She added the caption: "Turned out great!"

REVENGE BODY

The Sister Wives star and her ex-husband Kody Brown, 54, called it quits in December 2022 after nearly 30 years together.

In the months since, Janelle has focused primarily on her health, dropping over 100 pounds total.

Earlier this week, the blonde flaunted her major revenge body in a new video on her Instagram feed.

The reality star took to the platform on Thursday to share photos and videos while on a road trip with her youngest daughter, Savanah, 18.

The mother-daughter duo posed for a selfie while in the car, showing off their to-go diet juices for the ride.

In a second snap, Janelle posed alone, smiling brightly for the camera with her Plexus bottle.

The TLC star included a picture of the road ahead as she headed off to her daughter Maddie's house.

She sipped her pink juice while driving her Lexus down the highway.

The mother of six looked thinner than ever in the photoset, showing off her slim face and figure to fans.

Janelle captioned her post: "What a great road trip to Maddie's. I'll say they get easier and easier, especially when I'm giving my body what it needs.

"Balancing blood sugar, sustainable energy without caffeine spikes, and helping with any brain fog or lag, I might experience. Message if you're ready to feel your best, too!

"@life_with_health_and_happiness has more info on our inflammation challenge if you tend to carry inflammation weight, aches in joints...etc," she closed.
