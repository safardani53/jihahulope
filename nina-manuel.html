Nina Manuel Biography

Publish date: 2024-02-12

She is a married woman. She got married to Luv Shah. He is New York based businessman. They got married with Hindu as well as Christian rituals. Their Hindu wedding was performed at the JW Marriott in Juhu, Mumbai and a Church wedding at St. Peter's Church, Bandra  and that was in 2010. They are still together and spending good time together. There is no any sign of getting them divorce yet. Besides this there is no any information about her love affair and boyfriend.

She completed her schooling in Dubai. She completed her graduation from Mithibai College in commerce. She took part in Femina Look of the Year contest in 1995. She didn’t win but from that point her modeling career began. She began her modeling career with a show for Ensemble, an haute couture store, and got picked up by India's top designers.  Talking about her television career she has hosted - Nina at 9 on Zee Muzic, Midnite Manuel on Zee Muzic, After Hours on Zee Cafe and many more. She earns massive amount of salary and her net worth is not revealed yet.
