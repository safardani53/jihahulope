Mom Arrested For Throwing Teen Daughter a Naked Twister Party; Also Had Sex With Teen's Boyfriend

Publish date: 2024-08-23

So much for a pony and a clown.

A Georgia mother of five was arrested last Saturday, April 11, after she admitted throwing a naked Twister party for her 16-year-old daughter.

Rachel Lynn Lehnardt of Evans, Ga., disclosed disturbing details of the naked Twister party to her Alcoholics Anonymous sponsor during a sobriety plan meeting.

Thankfully, her sponsor responded by calling the authorities.

According to AugustaCrime (dot) com, Lehnardt gave her 16-year-old permission to have a party that included a naked twister game, drinking alcohol, smoking weed.  

Lehnardt, 35, also partook in the naked Twister game.

The sheriff reported that Lehnardt had sex with one of the 18-year-old guests in the bathroom while the rest of the kids played naked Twister.

It seems impossible that this party could get any worse.

But it does, and considerably so. Lehnardt also disclosed that during the party she was awakened by someone having sex with her.

She thought it was the 18-year-old she hooked up with in the bathroom … but it was actually her 16-year-old daughter’s boyfriend.

This could possibly lead to rape charges for the 16-year-old.  However, Lehnardt refuses to discuss the incident, so authorities cannot press charges.

And if it were consensual, the age of consent in Georgia is 16.

On Monday, she was charged with two counts of contribution to the delinquency of a minor for providing alcohol and marijuana. 

She was released after posting a $3,200 bond.

Clearly, this isn’t the first time Lehnardt has made terrible decisions as a parent. On April 6, she lost custody of her children – ages 4, 6, 8, 10, and 16 – because of a separate incident.

The incident that resulted in the loss of custody was not reported.

I think the only thing left to say is WTF?!?!?!?
