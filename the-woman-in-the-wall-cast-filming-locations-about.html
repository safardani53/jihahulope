The Woman in the Wall cast, filming locations, start date: All about new BBC drama

Publish date: 2024-06-29

FANS of Ruth Wilson are thrilled that the award-winning actress is back with a brand new thrilling drama.

Called The Woman in the Wall, this gripping BBC One series follows a woman who wakes up to find a dead body in her house.

Who is in The Woman in the Wall cast?

Ruth Wilson as Lorna Brady

Acclaimed actress Ruth Wilson plays Lorna Brady, a woman plagued by a childhood trauma that affects her adult life.

Speaking about the role, she told the BBC: "Lorna Brady is a complex and fascinating character and I’m thrilled to help bring her to life.

"In The Woman in the Wall Joe Murtagh has created both an enthralling gothic thriller and a moving examination of the legacy of The Magdalene Laundries.

"It’s a privilege to bring this story to screens.”

Ruth has seen her star soar over the last decade, and is known for high profile shows like His Dark Materials, The Affair, Luther, and Mrs Wilson.

Daryl McCormack

Fresh from starring in Bad Sisters, actor Daryl McCormack is once again playing a man on a mission.

In The Woman in the Wall, he takes on the role of Detective Colman Akande, who is described as "ambitious" and someone who "rose through the ranks of the Garda Síochána thanks to his natural aptitude".

His character's scathing wit hides a quiet sadness, and when he meets Lorna Brady, he finds himself forced to confront his own haunting secrets.

The rest of the cast includes:

  • Chizzy Akudolu plays Lola Akande - the actress is known for Holby City.
  • Simon Delaney plays Sgt. Aidan Massey - the actor is known for Inside Man.
  • Lynn Rafferty plays Anna - the actress is known for North Sea Connection.
  • Fiona Browne plays Margaret Brady - the actress is known for Vikings: Valhalla.

What is The Woman in the Wall about?

Lorna Brady is a woman from the small, fictional Irish town of Kilkinure who wakes one morning to find a corpse in her house. Chillingly,

She has no idea who the dead woman is, or if she herself might be responsible for the apparent murder.

That’s because Lorna has long suffered from extreme bouts of sleepwalking ever since she was ripped from her life at the age of 15 and incarcerated in a convent.

There, she gave birth to her daughter Agnes, who was cruelly taken from her and whose fate Lorna has never known.

Unluckily for Lorna, Detective Colman Akande is on her tail, for a crime seemingly unrelated to the body she has discovered in her house.

Where was the Woman in the Wall filmed?

Filming took place in Northern Ireland in 2022.

The Woman in the Wall takes place in the fictional town of Kilkinure, but is set in the real life County Mayo.

Creator and Executive Producer Joe Murtagh revealed the reason why this area was used.

Speaking to the BBC he said: “My family is from Mayo, the county in which the fictional Kilkinure is set, and it deeply frustrates and saddens me that it feels so few people have heard of the Laundries that existed across Ireland.

"I hope that by making something that has the familiarity of a genre piece we are able to shed some light on the awful things that occurred within these kind of institutions and introduce this history to the wider public, so that nothing like it may ever happen again.” 

When does The Woman in the Wall start?

The Woman in the Wall will start on Sunday, August 27, 2023, at 9pm on BBC One.
