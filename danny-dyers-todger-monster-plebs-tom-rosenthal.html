Danny Dyer's todger is 'a monster' he likes to whip out on set reveals Plebs co-star Tom Rosenthal

Publish date: 2024-07-25

DANNY Dyer’s todger is “a monster” according to his Plebs co-star Tom Rosenthal.

The actor, who appeared alongside the hardman back in 2013, opened up about the eye-opening scene which saw Danny strip off, during an appearance on The Chris Ramsey Show.

He said: "I'm sure there's been a lot of big revelations in your basement here, Chris Ramsay, but Danny has a massive… he is big."

The star, who also appears in Friday Night Dinner, continued: "I don't want to be crass, but in the competition between our genitalia, I finish third, do you know what I mean? Huge. Monster of a thing."

Wanting to know more, host Chris asked: "Was he proud of showing it off?"

Tom revealed: "Yeah! He loved it. It was so weird, it was all out and stuff, but he was worried it wasn't big enough so he kept trying to manipulate it to make it look bigger.

"At one point he started helicoptering it round."

EastEnders actor Danny has previously boasted about having a massive testicle.

Back in 2016 he shocked Holly Willoughby during an appearance on Celebrity Juice when whipped it out to prove a point.

When asked if the rumours about it were true, he said: “I got one massive b*****k yeh one massive b*****k. It’s a mad thing yeah. It weighs a f***ing ton. I’d love to whack it out. Can I whack it out?

One co-star who isn’t so sure about his claims though is Danny-Boy Hatchard, who played Lee Carter on the soap.
