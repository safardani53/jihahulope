Taylor Swift Spends Hours with Cancer Patients in Providence, Continues To Be Awesome

Publish date: 2024-04-01

For the second time in a week, Taylor Swift has dedicated a chunk of her time to hospitalized cancer patients.

Just days after a spokesperson for New York’s Memorial Sloan-Kettering Cancer Center confirmed that Swift engaged in one of the longest celebrities visit on record, the singer did the very same thing at Hasbro Children’s Hospital in Providence.

Accompanied by her father, Swift hung around for five hours, meeting one-on-one with kids and playing a song on her guitar for those in the psychiatric unit.
