Britney Spears Gets Rainbow Hair Extensions: Love It or Hate It?

Publish date: 2024-09-16

Things are looking bright for X Factor judge Britney Spears — literally!

The “Oops!…I Did It Again” pop star, 30, rocked pink and blue hair extensions when she joined Ryan Seacrest, 37, on stage during the iHeartRadio Music Festival in Las Vegas September 21. Spears showed off her trim figure in a black mini-dress by David Lerner and glitter platform heels by Charlotte Olympia.

“Hello, Las Vegas!” a beaming Spears said on stage. “You know why I love this festival. It’s packed with every kind of music fan.” (Performers included Jason Aldean, Rihanna and Bon Jovi, among others.)

Spears added, “This is Vegas, baby, so we’ve got the world’s most insane deejays here to keep the party going all night long.”
