Beloved BBC Breakfast presenter STILL missing from show as familiar face forced to step in

Publish date: 2024-04-20

A BELOVED BBC Breakfast presenter was still missing from the show today as a familiar face was forced to step in.

Jon Kay was on hand to present the first show of the new week this morning, but his usual co-host Sally Nugent was nowhere to be seen.

Sally, 52, had hosted the programme alone on New Year's Day last week, before being absent on Tuesday and Wednesday.

Instead, Jon was joined by Sarah Campbell, who had also hosted the show with him last week in Sally's absence.

BBC Breakfast fans were delighted to see her on the red sofa, with one writing on X: "morning, getting regular on the sofa."
