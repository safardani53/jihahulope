A MINDBOGGLING new challenge has left puzzlers across the internet scratching their heads.

Can you spot all 22 objects hiding in this busy winter-themed optical illusion?

Jo Ana, who lives in Lithuania, designed the challenge as a reminder of all the social activities that can be enjoyed this season.

Many people are looking for creative ways to have fun now that the party season is over but the weather is still gloomy.

While the ice skaters are busy drawing patterns in the ice with their skates, the below items are hidden in plain sight: 

A snow sled, owl, house, eye, flower, tree in a window, mug, kite, carrot, person kissing, top hat, butterfly, magazine, hand, star, hearts, ladybug, three-leaf clover, gift, book.

Spotting the owl makes you feel like you’re on a roll, however, not all items are as easy to find. 

Only the sharpest minds can spot all 22 hidden objects in 30 seconds or less.

How many of the items have you found so far?

The carrot likely stands out to you as the lady’s skirt is very eye-catching, and even the eye appears to be looking right at you.
