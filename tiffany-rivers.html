‘Change the Game’ Diabetes Awareness Campaign

As if the couple was not loved enough already, they have set another thing to like about them.

“Hermoza is partnering with these three incredible diabetes charities this month to raise funds and awareness for a cause near and dear to us,” Marisa said.

“At Hermoza, we place a high emphasis on making the world a better place and are taking care of all hard costs, so every dollar raised through t-shirt and hat sales goes directly to these organizations. Every donation received creates an opportunity for making a difference and helping those in need.”

Many might not know this, but their son, Gunner, was diagnosed with Type 1 Diabetes at five, changing their lives forever.

“I want to change the game not only for my son but for others with diabetes.” Philip Rivers said in the video.

“My son was diagnosed with T1D at age five and we have watched him live his life to the fullest every day since. We are thankful for the incredible diabetes organizations and experts, and hope our efforts make a difference in the lives of others living with this disease.”

Furthermore, they also emphasized how their line, Hermoza, was trying to make the world a better place.
