Who Is Susana Gomez? All About Malumas Girlfriend Hollywood Life

Publish date: 2024-05-05

Maluma is going to be a dad for the first time! The “11PM” singer, 29, revealed that his girlfriend Susana Gómez is pregnant in his music video for “Procura,” which dropped on Thursday, October 19. The rapper and singer, whose real name is Juan Luis Londoño Arias, announced the exciting news when he premiered the music video at his Washington D.C. concert. The clip features the two of them in home videos throughout their pregnancy journey. The video also revealed that they’re having a baby girl. As they prepare to begin the adventure of parenthood, get to know Maluma’s long-term partner here!

Maluma Has Been Dating Susana Gómez Since 2020

While it’s not entirely clear when and how Maluma first met Susana, romance rumors about the pair first surfaced in 2020, per Hola! They were first seen together in August of that year. Over a year after the rumors began, Maluma seemed to go public with the relationship in a since-deleted photo on Instagram of them kissing at Christmas in December 2021. “Gracias Santa,” he wrote, per People. 

Fans Think the Song “Junio” Is About Her

While Junio has plenty of love songs in his catalog, many fans believe that his 2022 track was inspired by his love for Susana. The sensual track has tons of romantic lines, and the rapper even gave Susana a kiss after performing the track at the 2022 Latin Billboard Music Awards. “The moon hid right when it saw you/Because you shine more than her,” he sings at the opening of the song in Spanish, per Billboard.

She’s An Architect and Designer

While Maluma is a huge star out of the Latin music scene, Susana clearly prefers to stay mostly out of the spotlight. Her Instagram is private, but she have a tag for her jewelry brand Sileo Timeless Jewelry, which she launched in February 2023. Besides her jewelry line, she’s also an architect and interior designer, showing that she’s incredibly multi-talented.

Maluma Dated Natalia Barulich from 2018 to 2019

Prior to meeting Susana, Maluma had a nearly two-year-long relationship with model Natalia Barulich. The pair met in 2017, when she starred in his “Felices Los 4” music video, and they started dating soon after. Maluma went public with their relationship in January 2018, but the relationship ended by October 2019.

After splitting up, Natalia opened up about their relationship in a June 2020 episode of the Evolve podcast, per Hola! “I was giving 1000% and I was only receiving 20%, I would say that some days I got more and that was what made me stay,” she said before opening up about her feelings on the breakup. “There were days when I felt stronger than others, then I felt sad, missing the relationship in some way, but for me it was very toxic.”
