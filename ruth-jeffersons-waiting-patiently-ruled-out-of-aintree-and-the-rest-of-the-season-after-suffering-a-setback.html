Publish date: 2024-05-27

WAITING PATIENTLY has been ruled out for the rest of the season after suffering a setback.

Ruth Jefferson's star chaser had been set to run at Aintree, but connections have had to rule him out of the race and the rest of the year.

Waiting Patiently was last seen beating Cue Card at Ascot.

"Unfortunately we've had a setback and it's come at the wrong time, with Aintree around the corner," said the North Yorkshire handler.

"He definitely won't run again this season and we're just waiting back for more detail from the vet to see exactly what it is."
