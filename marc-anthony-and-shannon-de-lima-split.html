Marc Anthony and Shannon de Lima Split

Publish date: 2024-02-07

Singer Marc Anthony is back on the market. The Latin singer and his girlfriend of one year, model Shannon de Lima, have called it quits.

Marc’s rep Blanca Lasalle told PEOPLE, “Marc is no longer dating model Shannon de Lima. The amicable breakup became official in mid-January.”

A source familiar with the couple said, “The truth is they have been having a great time together, but it no longer worked. Marc is in a good place. Sometimes things work out and sometimes they don’t.”

Just a week ago the two were seen looking cozy in New York City at Marquee. A patron said, “Shannon was dancing up a storm with friends while sipping champagne and taking iPhone photos of the light show. At one point, Shannon even jokingly gave Marc a lap dance when Pitbull came on.”

According to Page Six, he had a meeting with his ex-wife Jennifer Lopez at The Mark Hotel in Manhattan Tuesday. She entered his hotel wearing thigh-high stiletto boots and a leopard-print micro-mini and emerged two and a half hours later.

He exited the building not long afterward and got on his waiting tour bus. It was likely a mixed discussion of their mutual business interests and their two children, twins Max and Emme.

Not along for her quick trip to the Big Apple was her backup dancer boyfriend Casper Smart.
