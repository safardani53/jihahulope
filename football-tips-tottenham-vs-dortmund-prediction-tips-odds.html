Tottenham vs Dortmund - Prediction, tips and odds

WEMBLEY should play host to a Champions League classic tonight.

Spurs are flying despite their injury problems while Borussia Dortmund sit top of the Bundesliga.

And the tie is all about the homecoming of England starlet Jadon Sancho.

Sancho has quickly become Dortmund’s key man and he’s 10/1 with Betway to score and assist.

Both are brimming with attacking options despite injury issues plaguing either side so we’ll back both teams to score at 4/6 with Sport Pesa.

And we fancy Heung-min Son to score first at 6/1 with William Hill after bagging eight in nine games against Dortmund throughout his career.
