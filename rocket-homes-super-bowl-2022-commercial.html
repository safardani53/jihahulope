Who stars in the Rocket Homes Super Bowl 2022 commercial?

Publish date: 2024-07-09

BUSINESSES everywhere produce their most memorable advertisements just for the annual Super Bowl.

Ahead of Super Bowl 2022, Rocket Homes and Mortgage released a sneak preview of their commercial which features a couple of familiar faces.

Who stars in the Rocket Homes Super Bowl 2022 commercial?

On February 7, Mortgage loan specializer Rocket Homes and Mortgage provided viewers a sneak peek of their commercial for Super Bowl 2022 on YouTube.

The short clip features actress Anna Kendrick nervously making conversation with an unseen figure.

"I'm so excited we're in the Rocket Super Bowl commercial together, I've just loved you since I was a kid," Anna begins.

Anna nervously quips: "Not that you're old, you look great, not that it's important ma'am."

She adds: "Ma'am? Miss? I'm sorry I just don't know what to call you."

The camera pans to Barbie in a make-up chair, who responds to the actress: "Oh, just call me Barbie!"
