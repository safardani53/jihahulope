Next shoppers go wild for the new-in Myleene Klass dresses that are perfect for a Summer holiday

Publish date: 2024-05-18

NEXT shoppers are going wild for the new-in Myleene Klass dresses, which are not only stunning, but are brilliant for a Summer holiday too.

So if your wardrobe needs a refresh for the warmer weather, or you’ve got a holiday booked in the coming months, you’ll want to listen up and head down to your nearest Next as quickly as you can.

Posting on their official Instagram account, @nextofficial, the high-street chain shared a clip of Myleene Klass, the British musician, television presenter and model, modelling two of her new dresses from her Next range.

Myleene, who was a member of pop group Hear/Say, now has a stunning women’s clothing range with Next, which shoppers have been raving about on social media.

In the clip, we saw Myleene model two printed wrap dresses from her edit, which contains a variety of Summer staples - including Summer dresses and swimwear, as well as party frocks and sparkly jumpsuits.

In the video, Mylenne swung around a post, showing off the two dresses - one in pink and orange and another with a blue floral tropical print.

Next captioned the clip ‘Which is your favourite?’ and shoppers were quick to flood the comments, revealing which dress they preferred best from the I’m A Celebrity South Africa star’s range.

While the shoppers loved both looks, fashionistas overwhelmingly preferred the pink printed dress. 

One person said: “The pink one is my favourite.” 

Another added: “Love pink and red combo.”

A third commented: “Pink and red but I don’t know if I’d be brave enough to go so bold.” 

Whilst another shopper noted: “Wow, beautiful wrap dresses!”

At the same time, one fashion fan posted: "The blue dress is the cutest."

Meanwhile, another Next shopper simply said: "Stunning."

If you fancy getting your hands on these beautiful dresses, they are available to order online, or to buy in store now.

But you’ll need to be quick, as sizes are selling out fast.

Particularly in the pink and orange dress, many shoppers have been keen to snap it up.

The dresses are £55 each and range from size 6 to a size 22.
