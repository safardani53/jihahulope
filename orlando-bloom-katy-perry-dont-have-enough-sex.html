Orlando Bloom admits he and Katy Perry 'don't have enough sex' after birth of daughter Daisy, 7 mont

Publish date: 2024-03-08

Orlando Bloom divulged he and his fiancée, Katy Perry "don't have enough sex."

The father made his candid remarks to The Guardian following the birth of daughter Daisy, seven months.

A reporter asked Orlando: “How often do you [and Katy] have sex?”

The new dad responded: “Not enough—we just had a baby, though."

He and Katy welcomed Daisy Dove back in August.

He called his son 10-year-old son Flynn, whom he shares with ex Miranda Kerr, his late dog Mighty, and Daisy, the greatest loves of his life.

Mighty, the poodle, tragically died over the summer apparently after a coyote took him.

Orlando memorialized his beloved pet by tattooing the late pooch's name on his chest.

He said in the interview: “I lost my poodle, Mighty. He was out of my sight for just seven minutes; he went out on an adventure and didn’t come back, and was taken by a coyote, I think.

“It was awful, really painful; he taught me about love and loyalty, and how the connection between living beings can be.”

The new parents' lives are set to only get busier as Katy is reportedly launching a Las Vegas residency later this year.

According to Billboard, the 36-year-old will be performing at the brand-new Resorts World resort casino in Sin City.

The pop star will apparently start performing toward the end of 2021 and run through 2022.

Resorts World is the first casino to be built in Las Vegas for over a decade, and the indoor stage is expected to seat 5,000 people.
