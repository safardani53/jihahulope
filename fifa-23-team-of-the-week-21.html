Saka gets a huge buff in FIFA 23s Team of the Week 21

Publish date: 2024-09-26

FIFA 23’s has upped its game, as Team of the Week (TOTW) features three double-boosted cards for the first time.

Young Player of the Year Buyako Saka also managed to make his way into the top cut earning a three-point boost.

The highest-rated players only reach 88, but there are four of them on the roster this week.

Frenkie de Jong, Douglas Luiz, and Viktor Tsygankov all received a one-point boost from 87 to 88.

Victor Oshmhen got a larger boost of five points to move from 83 to 88.

There are three double boosts cards this week, which is a first for TOTW.

More in FIFA 23

Steve Mandanda has received a six-point boost, from 81 to 87.

Exequiel Palacios has received a large boost of nine points, from 78 to 87.

Thiago Almada receives the biggest boost of 10 points this week, from 76 to 86.

These are some of the highest-rated cards in FIFA Ultimate Team (FUT) this week, with huge boosts overall.

Most read in Gaming

If you download the FIFA 23 companion web or mobile app, you can open FIFA Ultimate Team (FUT) packs on the go.

This means you can start building your team ready to play in the game.

The TOTW cards will show up as special cards in FUT packs this week, with boosted stats.

This week’s team will be available via FUT packs until Wednesday, March 29, 5pm UK time.

After that, these boosted cards will only be purchasable via the transfer market, usually for high prices.

Here is a list of all the players in FIFA 23’s Team of the Week and their boosted stats:

  • 88 - De Jong, Douglas Luiz, Tsygankov, Osimhen
  • 87 - Mandanda, Palacios
  • 86 - Almada
  • 85 - Haller, Saka, Kramarić, Kleindienst
  • 84 - Estupiñán, Maripán, Tella, Zaccagni
  • 83 - Rønnow, Nyamsi, Petratos, Geertruida
  • 81 - Austin, Itten
  • 79 - Pollock, Layouni
