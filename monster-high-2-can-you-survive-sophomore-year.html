Monster High 2 - Can You Survive Sophomore Year?

Publish date: 2024-05-19
Can you boo-lieve it? Monster High 2, the sequel to the live-action movie musical based on the iconic Monster High franchise, is reuniting everyone’s favorite beasties, er … besties. In the new movie, now streaming on Paramount+, Clawdeen Wolf (Miia Harris), Draculaura (Nayah Damasen), and Frankie Stein (Ceci Balagot) are all back for their second year at Monster High. But it’s not long before this power of three is imperiled by the arrival of new students, new abilities, and an even bigger threat that could destroy not only their friendship, but the world as they know it. Will these BFFs, and their fellow Monster High ghouls make it through their sophomore year?
