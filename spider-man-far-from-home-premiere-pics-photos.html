Far From Home Premiere Pics Hollywood Life

Publish date: 2024-06-03

The ‘Spider-Man: Far From Home’ premiere took place at the TCL Chinese Theatre in Los Angeles on June 26, and more than one star dressed to theme! Zendaya graced the red carpet in a backless gown covered in red and black sequins, while her co-star Tom Holland stepped out in a maroon suit with black accents on the collar.

More Marvel stars made their way down the red carpet, like Jake Gyllenhaal, Samuel L. Jackson, Marisa Tomei, Cobie Smulders, Jacob Batalon and Martin Starr.

‘Spider-Man: Far From Home’ premieres in the United States on July 2, 2019, and there’s extra excitement surrounding this latest installment in the ‘Spider-Man’ franchise. It’s the first film in the MCU to be released after ‘Avengers: Endgame,’ which changed the course for all Marvel movies to follow. The movie, directed by Jon Watts and co-written by Chris McKenna and Erik Sommers, serves as the follow-up to ‘Spider-Man: Homecoming.’
