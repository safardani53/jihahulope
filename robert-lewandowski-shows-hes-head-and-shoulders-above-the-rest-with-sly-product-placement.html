Robert Lewandowski shows he's Head and Shoulders above the rest... with sly product placement

Publish date: 2024-02-04

ROBERT LEWANDOWSKI shows why he is Head and Shoulders above the rest with this sneaky snap.

The Bayern Munich striker posted a photo on Instagram after he had just finished training.

And it does not take the sharpest of eyes to see the rather blatant product placement on show - as well as a missing lightbulb.

No wonder he is a sponsor's dream. It was just this week he was involved in a Nike photoshoot with the likes of Marcus Rashford and Harry Kane.

Lewa wrote: "Done with last training. Tomorrow we are going back to the pitch! Time to start rematch round."

But behind the Poland star's head, clearly in view, is some Head and Shoulders shampoo.

And yes, of course, Lewa is sponsored by them.

Although, judging by his grey streaks, he may want to consider some Just for Men.

Head and Shoulders have quite a thing for footballers. Who can forget Joe Hart's flake-free adverts?

They have also had Lewandowski's team-mate Manuel Neuer, Lionel Messi and Iker Casillas, as well as former Formula One world champion Sebastian Vettel.

Bundesliga leaders Bayern Munich travel to SC Freiburg tomorrow in their second match back following the winter break.
