Star Wars Dress Is a Fanboy Favorite at Met Gala

Publish date: 2024-02-08

Many attendees of the 2014 MET Gala clearly felt they were killin’ it with their fashion choices, but only one starlet had the guts to rock a dress that could blow up entire planets.

Yes, that’s the Death Star on Kirsten Dunst’s dress, known to Star Wars enthusiasts the world over as Darth Vader’s eternally-renovated headquarters. 

Kirsten, of course, is best known for her role as Mary Jane Watson in Sam Raimi’s Spider-Man films, meaning that sound you hear is millions of fanboys panting at the thought of having a chance with Dunst.

 

So does this mean Kirsten is as excited as the rest of us about the announcement of the Episode VII cast?

Or maybe she’s just still celebrating May the Fourth.

Either way, Kirsten’s sexy/geeky ensemble certainly made the thoughts of some fans turn over to the Dark Side.

As a cherry on top of this nerd sundae, Kirsten was spotted chatting with Shailene Woodley who plays Mary Jane in the new Spider-Man franchise (her part was cut from the latest installment).

Kirsten seems to be running some sort of campaign for fanboy popularity. If you’re paying attention, J.J. Abrams, it’s not too late to cast her in Episode VII.
