Dreamville 2023: Who is in the lineup?

Publish date: 2024-07-20

J Cole's annual music festival, Dreamville, returns once more in April 2023.

Here's a rundown of the musical acts that'll perform this year.

Who is in the Dreamville 2023 lineup?

J Cole's Dreamville Festival is back for its second time around after a three-year hiatus.

The two-day event will run from April 1-2, 2023, at the Dorthea Dix Park in Raleigh, North Carolina.

Tickets have been available for purchase since November, however, J Cole only released his carefully picked lineup on February 28.

The artists set to perform are the following:

Sunday, April 2

Who is headlining Dreamville 2023?

On the final day of the festival, J Cole normally headlines the show. This time around, he'll be sharing the stage with "special guest," Drake.

Additionally, Burna Boy is set to headline before J Cole's performance.

On April 1, Usher will headline the Saturday event.

Normally, the headlining acts have an hour-long set starting at 9.45pm.

In 2022, J Cole and rapper Lil Baby headlined the two-day festival.

How can I buy tickets?

Tickets are available to purchase on the Dreamville Festival website.

According to the website, ticket prices range from $299.99 for general admission to $1499.99 for their MVP VIP package.

The festival is also offering shuttles from Downtown Raleigh to Dorthea Dix Park for $20.
