Barbara Palvin, born October 8, 1993, is a Hungarian model. She’s known for appearances in the Sports Illustrated Swimsuit Issues, which she first appeared in 2016, and Victoria’s Secret. The lingerie brand made her an official Angel in 2019. She was discovered at the age of 13 by a modeling scout and has since covered  L’Officiel (Paris, Russia, Turkey, Thailand, Singapore), Vogue (Portugal), Marie Claire (Italy, Hungary), Glamour Hungary, Elle (Britain, Italy, Korea, Brazil, Argentina, Sweden, Serbia, Hungary), Allure, Harper’s Bazaar and Jealouse Magazine. Barbara has appeared in campaigns for high end brands such as Armani Exchange, and H&M. In February 2012, she became an ambassador for L’Oréal Paris.
