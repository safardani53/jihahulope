Who is Ryan Gosling?

Ryan Gosling is a famous Canadian actor who has taken Hollywood by storm with his stellar performances.

He won four Teen Choice Awards in 2005 for his role in ‘The Notebook’, including Choice Movie Breakout Performance – Male.

In 2006, he received the Best Actor Award at the Las Palmas Film Festival for the movie, ‘Half Nelson’.

Early Life of Ryan Gosling 

Ryan Thomas Gosling was born on the 12th of November 1980. He is 42 years old as of 2022. His zodiac sign is Sagittarius.

He was born in Ontario, Canada. He holds a Canadian nationality and belongs of Irish American descent.

He and his family were members of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints and has said that the religion influenced every aspect of their lives. Ryan is a devout Christian.

He was born to Thomas Ray Gosling, a traveling salesman, and Donna, his mother, a secretary who later became a teacher. He has one elder sister.

His parents divorced when he was a child and he lived with his mother and sister.

He went to Cornwall Collegiate and Vocational School before going to Lester B. Pearson High School. He was diagnosed with attention deficit hyperactivity disorder (ADHD) and thus his mother quit her job and homeschooled him.

He loved movies and performing from a young age. When he was 12, he auditioned for a part in ‘The Mickey Mouse Club’ and signed a contract for two years. Along with his sister, he used to sing at weddings and he also joined a local ballet company.

Ryan Gosling Career 

Starting his career as a child actor, he appeared in the teenage comedy series ‘Breaker High’ from 1997 to 1998. He quit high school and moved to Los Angeles to appear as the title character in the teen series ‘Young Hercules’.

In 2000, he played a supporting role in the football drama, ‘Remember the Titans’ which led to a leading role as a Jew in the film ‘The Believer’ in 2001.

He appeared along with Michael Pitt and Sandra Bullock in the psychological thriller, ‘Murder By Numbers’ in which he played a wealthy high schooler who attempts to commit the perfect murder.

He found his breakthrough role in 2004 when he was cast as Noah in the romantic drama film, ‘The Notebook’, opposite Rachel McAdams.

The film follows the story of a couple who fall in love during the early 1940s. The movie was a big commercial hit and helped establish Gosling as an actor.

In 2006, he appeared in the drama film ‘Half Nelson’ which revolved around the story of a school teacher who forms a relationship with one of his students. Gosling was nominated for an Academy Award for his role in this critically acclaimed film.

His role as Lars in the 2007 comedy-drama ‘Lars and the Real Girl’ was critically appreciated.

Gosling is also a musician. He formed a rock duo with Zach Shields in 2007 and released their first album, ‘Dead Man’s Bones’ in 2009.

In 2010, he appeared in the romantic drama ‘Blue Valentine’ which follows the story of a married couple from the time of their courtship to the dissolution of their marriage several years later.

He appeared along with Steve Carell and Julianne Moore in the romantic comedy-drama ‘Crazy, Stupid, Love’ in 2011.

He played the role of Sergeant Jerry Wooters in the action crime ‘Gangster Squad’ in 2013 which was loosely based on the story of Los Angeles Police Department officers and detectives.

In 2014, he dabbled into the field of direction and his debut venture ‘Lost River’ competed in the Un Certain Regard section at the 2014 Cannes Film Festival.

He is involved with various charities including PETA and Invisible Children Inc.

Ryan Gosling Personal Life 

Ryan Gosling and Eva Mendes are one of the most private couples in Hollywood. Despite being together since 2011 and sharing two daughters, we weren’t even sure if they were married until November 2022.

He shares a home with his pet dog, George. His daughters are named Amada Lee, 6, and Esmeralda Amada, 8.

In the past, he had been linked with several women including Sandra Bullock and Rachel McAdams.

Since he was so secretive about his marriage, people could not actually figure it out that he even had kids with Eva Mendes.
