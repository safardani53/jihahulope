Who is Zakir Hussain:

Zakir Hussain is a famous Drummer. He was born on March 9, 1951 and his birthplace is India. Zakir is also well known as, Famous for his work on the tabla (an Indian percussion instrument), this Hindustani and jazz fusion musician was a 1999 recipient of the United States Endowment for the Arts’ prestigious National Heritage Fellowship. His other honors include the Sangeet Natak Akademi Award, the Padma Bhushan, and the Grammy Award for World Music.

Zakir is originated from India. He and American bassist Bill Laswell founded a world music band called Tabla Beat Science.
